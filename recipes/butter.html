Butter

Butter is one of the most basic, but best foods humans have ever made.

Many cultures have butter as of of their staple items.

Ingrediants

  • 1 Cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Bowl of ice cold water
  • Optional choices below
  • 2 TBSP Honey
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Add heavy whipping cream to mixing bowl and set on medium
  2. let run until solid and liquids start to seperate (~10mins)
  3. grab the solids, and in the ice cold bowl of water, start sqeezing out any excess fluid
  4. Once done with that, add the salt in
  5. If you are adding any of the addtional Ingrediants, do so now
