Bread

Bread is one of the most basic, but best foods humans have ever made.

Many cultures have bread as of of their staple items.

Ingrediants

  • 4 cups Flour
  • 1/2 cup warm Water
  • 1 packet Yeast
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 TBSP Honey

Instructions

  1. Mix honey and yeast into the warm water (100'F), cover, and let set for about 10 minutes, or until the yeast has started foaming.
  2. Add in the salt and flour - mix until no longer sticking to side of bowl
  3. Knead for 10-15 minutes, or until the dough is no longer sticky
  4. Set in greased bowl and cover. Let set for 1-2 hours, or until the douch has doubled in size
  5. Prehead oven to 400'F, put dough in pan
  6. Cook for 40 minutes
  7. Once out of the oven, put bread on cooling rack for an hour before cutting in
